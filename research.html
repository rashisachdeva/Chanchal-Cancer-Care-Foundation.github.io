Research & Academic Initiatives

At Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation, we believe that research and education are essential to improving cancer prevention and treatment.

Clinical Research Studies

We conduct research on early cancer detection, treatment effectiveness, and public health strategies. Our research initiatives aim to contribute to the global knowledge base on cancer prevention and management, particularly in resource-limited settings.

Current Focus: Early detection methods for breast and cervical cancer

Support Research

Medical Training Programs

We provide workshops and training for healthcare professionals on cancer diagnosis, treatment, and palliative care. Our comprehensive training programs help build capacity among medical professionals to better address cancer care needs.

Goal: Train 500 healthcare professionals annually

Publications and Reports

Publishing findings from our research to contribute to global cancer awareness and medical advancements. Our publications help disseminate valuable insights gained from our work in underserved communities.

Support Research

Student Internship and Fellowship Programs

Offering opportunities for students in medical and public health fields to gain hands-on experience in cancer care and community health. These programs help cultivate the next generation of healthcare professionals dedicated to cancer prevention and treatment.

Collaboration with Universities

Partnering with academic institutions for joint research projects and knowledge-sharing initiatives. These collaborations enhance our research capabilities and provide opportunities for academic exchange.
