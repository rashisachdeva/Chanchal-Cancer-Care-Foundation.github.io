About Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation

Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation is committed to reducing cancer-related mortality through early detection and preventive care. We focus on empowering communities with knowledge and resources to improve health outcomes. Our expert team of healthcare professionals, volunteers, and partners work tirelessly to make cancer screening accessible to underserved populations.

Our vision is a world where no woman succumbs to preventable cancers and no child suffers from anemia due to lack of awareness and screening. We aim to bridge the healthcare gap in rural and underserved areas by offering life-saving diagnostic services.
