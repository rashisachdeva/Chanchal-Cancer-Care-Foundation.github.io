About Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation

Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation is committed to reducing cancer-related mortality through early detection and preventive care. We focus on empowering communities with knowledge and resources to improve health outcomes and making cancer screening accessible to underserved populations.

Read more

Our Mission & Services

"Hope begins with awareness, Strength lies in care. Together, we fight the darkness, To bring light everywhere."

Free Screening Camps

Regular screening camps in underprivileged communities for breast, cervical, and oral cancer detection.

Counseling & Support Groups

Support groups and counseling sessions to help patients and families cope with diagnosis and treatment.

Financial Aid & Resource Assistance

Connecting patients with financial aid, crowdfunding, and government assistance programs.

Health & Nutrition for Children

Nutrition programs and iron supplementation drives to combat anemia in children.

Palliative Care Units

Compassionate care for terminally ill patients with focus on dignity, pain management and emotional support.

Onco-Day Care Facilities

Outpatient chemotherapy and supportive care for patients who don't require hospitalization.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH PROGRAMS

Our outreach programs aim to spread awareness, provide preventive healthcare, and support those in need. We conduct:

• School Health Programs

• Corporate Wellness Initiatives
• Free Health Checkup Camps

RESEARCH & ACADEMIC INITIATIVES

At Chanchal Cancer Care Foundation, we believe that research and education are essential to improving cancer prevention and treatment.

Clinical Research Studies

Conducting research on early cancer detection and public health strategies with a focus on breast and cervical cancer.

Medical Training Programs

Providing training for healthcare professionals on cancer diagnosis, treatment, and palliative care.
