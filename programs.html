Community Outreach Programs

Our outreach programs aim to spread awareness, provide preventive healthcare, and support those in need.

Door-to-Door Awareness Campaigns

Educating families about early cancer detection, self-examinations, and available screening services. Our volunteers visit homes to provide personalized education and encourage preventive healthcare practices.

Support Us

School Health Programs

Teaching children about the importance of nutrition, anemia prevention, and general health hygiene. Our interactive sessions make health education engaging and accessible for young minds.

Support Us

Women's Self-Help Groups

Empowering women with knowledge on reproductive health and cancer screening. These groups create safe spaces for women to discuss health concerns and learn about preventive care.

Support Us

Cancer Survivor Support Groups

Providing emotional and psychological support to those who have fought or are fighting cancer. These groups foster community, share experiences, and build resilience among survivors and their families.

Support Us

Corporate Wellness Initiatives

Partnering with workplaces to conduct employee health screenings and wellness programs. These initiatives promote a culture of health awareness and early detection in professional environments.

Support Us

Free Health Checkup Camps

Regular health camps in remote and rural areas offering free medical consultations and screenings. These camps bring essential healthcare services to communities with limited access to medical facilities.

OUR REACH

We are committed to expanding our healthcare services to reach more communities in need, with a focus on early detection and prevention.

Women's Health Initiatives

Screening for breast, cervical, and oral cancers in women across urban and rural areas.

Children's Health Programs

Conducting anemia screening and providing nutritional guidance for children.

Community Awareness Campaigns

Educating communities about the importance of early cancer detection.

Mobile Health Camps

Bringing healthcare to the doorstep of underserved populations.

Palliative Care Units

Providing compassionate care and support for terminally ill cancer patients, ensuring dignity and comfort in their final days.

Onco-Day Care Facilities

Offering outpatient chemotherapy, symptom management, and supportive care for cancer patients who do not require hospitalization.
